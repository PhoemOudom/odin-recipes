Avocado Salad

Description

This avocado salad is a delicious combination of ripe avocados, sweet onions,
fresh tomatoes, and cilantro. This recipe is so easy to make and very colorful —
I think you'll like it!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1 sweet onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 large ripe tomato, chopped
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste
